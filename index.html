Café & Restaurant Management Department

Overview

Café & Restaurant Management System The system is a fully integrated platform designed to manage all operational, financial, and inventory-related activities within a café or restaurant. It connects purchasing, stock control, recipe costing, POS operations, table management, billing, staff roles, and reporting into one unified, data-driven environment. This isn’t just a POS. It’s the operational brain of the restaurant.

Primary Goal

Provide a centralized, efficient, and accurate system that:
  • tracks stock and consumption
  • manages purchases and suppliers
  • processes orders and tables
  • calculates recipe cost and profitability
  • generates financial and operational reports
  • reduces waste, errors, and manual work Ultimately giving owners and managers full visibility over daily performance and profitability.

Core Modules

  • Supplier & Purchasing Management Handles supplier records, purchase invoices, payment tracking, and automatic stock updates. Stores supporting documents and evaluates supplier performance.
  • Inventory Management Real-time quantity tracking, low-stock alerts, stock transactions, daily consumption logging, waste tracking, and stock variance analysis.
  • Recipe Costing Each menu item is defined as a recipe containing ingredient quantities and costs. The system calculates the cost per item, profit margin, and updates pricing impact when ingredient costs change.
  • POS & Table Management Interactive table layout, open/close orders, transfer tables, merge/split bills, assign waiters, track dining duration, support Dine-In / Takeaway / Delivery workflows.
  • Billing & Discounts Order billing, printing or digital invoicing, percentage/fixed discounts, promo codes, happy-hour rules, and permission-based discount control.
  • Daily Consumption & Waste Analysis Records actual usage of raw materials, compares expected vs. actual consumption, highlights waste sources, and produces consumption insights.
  • Reporting & Analytics Comprehensive reports: purchases, stock movements, waste, recipe profitability, sales by item/table/waiter, discount usage, and daily/weekly/monthly revenue.

Target Users

  • Owners & managers (operations and profitability overview)
  • Accountants (purchases, payments, financials)
  • Storekeepers (stock and receiving)
  • Cashiers (billing)
  • Waiters (orders and tables)
Each role has permissions tailored to its responsibilities.

Technology & Architecture

I'm building the system using:

  • ADO.NET for maximum performance and full SQL control
  • Clean Architecture to decouple business rules, data access, and UI
  • Server for strong relational data modeling
  • Desktop application stack Avalonia UI

How the System Works

Purchases enter the system. Stock levels update automatically. Menu items consume inventory through recipes as orders are placed. Inventory decreases and transactions are logged. Reports analyze performance and financial outcomes. Every action leaves a data footprint in the database, powering real-time insights and accurate analytics.

Real Business Value

Restaurant owners care about practical answers:
  • Where is stock disappearing?
  • Why did waste spike today?
  • Why did latte cost increase suddenly?
  • Which items are most profitable?
  • Which suppliers cause delays?
  • How much cash went out today?
  • How much came in?
This system converts daily chaos into measurable, transparent data.

Summary

A data-driven operational platform that merges inventory, purchasing, POS, recipe costing, billing, and analytics into one ecosystem. It helps restaurants run faster, more accurately, and more profitably — with real visibility, not guesswork.
